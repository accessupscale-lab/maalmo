Trestle Scaffolding

Components

Trestle

Lightweight, foldable steel trestle frames designed for low-height work platforms, slab support and quick-deploy access scaffolding.

Key Highlights

  • Foldable, fast to deploy and store
  • Powder-coated or galvanized finish options
  • Stable A-frame geometry
  • Pairs with planks for instant work platforms
  • Lightweight yet load-rated
Request a QuoteView Specs

The Workhorse of Low-Height Access

Maalmo trestle frames are the everyday workhorse of finishing trades — painters, plasterers, electricians and ceiling crews. Foldable for quick mobility, robust enough for daily abuse, and pair-wise compatible with planks of varying lengths.

Technical Specifications

ParameterSpecification
Frame MaterialTubular steel, OD 32 mm × 2.5 mm
Standard Heights0.6 m / 0.9 m / 1.2 m / 1.5 m / 1.8 m
Folded Width~80 mm
