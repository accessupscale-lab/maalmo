BNM (Block N Mesh)

A durable steel block-and-mesh shuttering system engineered for slab and wall casting. Fast assembly, clean concrete finish and high reuse cycles.

Key Highlights

  • Modular block & mesh design — accommodates any plan
  • Reusable for 100+ casting cycles
  • Smooth concrete finish — minimal post-processing
  • Fast strip and shift
  • Compatible with adjustable posts and shoring
Request a QuoteView Specs

Smart Shuttering for Faster Cycles

BNM combines steel mesh panels with modular blocks to deliver a flexible, repeatable shuttering system. Crews can reconfigure for slabs, beams or walls — and the steel construction means panels survive far more pours than plywood-based alternatives.

Technical Specifications

ParameterSpecification
Panel MaterialSteel mesh + framed block
Panel SizesModular — custom configurations available
Reuse Cycles100+ pours under proper care
Concrete PressureUp to 60 kN/m² (configuration dependent)
Surface FinishPlain or textured options
