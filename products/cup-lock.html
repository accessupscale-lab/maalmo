Scaffolding System

Cup Lock System

A globally proven modular scaffolding system, the Cup Lock connects ledgers and standards through a unique top-cup, bottom-cup node — enabling fast, safe and tool-light assembly.

Key Highlights

  • Up to 4 ledgers per node — true modular flexibility
  • Tool-light assembly using a single hammer
  • Hot-dip galvanized for outdoor longevity
  • Compatible with international scaffolding standards
  • Reusable across project after project
Request a Quote View Specs

Built for Speed and Safety

Maalmo's Cup Lock scaffolding is engineered around a deceptively simple idea: a steel cup, hammered down, locks ledger blades into place — with up to four ledgers connected at every node. The result is a scaffold that goes up faster, comes down faster and stays safer in between.

Every component is fabricated from prime steel, welded under controlled conditions and hot-dip galvanized for site durability. Whether you're scaffolding a 40-storey tower or a refinery shutdown, our Cup Lock system performs.

Why Contractors Choose Maalmo Cup Lock

  • Faster Build Cycles — Up to 30% time savings vs. tube-and-clamp.
  • Lower Labour Cost — Single-hammer assembly, smaller crews.
  • Engineered Loads — Tested vertical and lateral capacities.
  • Long Service Life — Galvanized for years of reuse.
