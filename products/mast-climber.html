Mast Climber

A powered vertical access platform that climbs along a fixed mast — providing safe, level work platforms at any height for facade construction, cladding, painting and high-rise maintenance.

Key Highlights

  • Powered electric drive — climb to working level on demand
  • Modular mast extends to 100m+ height
  • High payload capacity for crew and material
  • Built-in fall arrest and overload protection
  • Safer and faster than conventional swing scaffolds
Request a QuoteView Specs

Engineered for Productive Heights

The Maalmo Mast Climber transforms how facade work happens. Instead of crews working from fixed scaffolds across an entire elevation, a powered platform brings the work face to whatever height is currently being built — at full safety, full capacity and full speed.

Why Mast Climber

  • Save Time — No tying-in, no daily climbing.
  • Save Material — A fraction of the steel of full-elevation scaffolding.
  • Improve Safety — Stable platform with rails, fall arrest and overload trip.
