Adjustable Post

Heavy-duty adjustable steel props (acrow / shoring posts) engineered for slab support, temporary load distribution and shoring during construction.

Key Highlights

  • Telescopic adjustment for any required height
  • Heavy-gauge steel — high vertical load rating
  • Rapid pin & collar adjustment
  • Galvanized or painted finish options
  • Compatible with U-heads and base plates
