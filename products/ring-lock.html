Ring Lock Scaffolding System

Scaffolding System

Ring Lock System

A multi-directional modular scaffolding system. The Ring Lock rosette accepts up to eight ledger or diagonal connections at a single node, unlocking complex geometries and heavier loads.

Key Highlights

  • 8-way rosette node — true 3D scaffolding
  • Wedge-locked connections for instant rigidity
  • Higher load capacity per standard
  • Hot-dip galvanized for site durability
  • Compatible with circular and irregular structures
Request a QuoteView Specs

Engineered for Complex Geometry

Where Cup Lock connects four ledgers, Ring Lock connects up to eight — making it ideal for round structures, irregular plans, heavy industrial scaffolding and high-load shoring towers.

Wedge-locked rosette joints deliver instant, vibration-proof rigidity. Every component is hot-dip galvanized, marked for traceability and tested to international scaffolding codes.

Why Ring Lock

  • Higher Loads — Suited to shoring and heavy industry.
  • 3D Flexibility — Round, polygon and irregular plans.
  • Fast Assembly — Wedge-lock = single hammer strike.
  • Long Life — Galvanized for years of reuse.
