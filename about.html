Our Story

Forging Industrial Progress, One Component at a Time.

Founded with a simple conviction — that industrial-grade steel deserves industrial-grade engineering — Maalmo Steels has grown into one of India's most trusted B2B manufacturers for scaffolding, fabrication and surface-finishing services.

Our journey began on a single production floor. Today, we operate a vertically integrated facility that houses design, laser cutting, forming, welding, galvanizing, blasting and painting — all under one roof. This control means our customers get predictable quality, on time, every time.

  • 25+ years experience
  • ISO 9001:2015 certified
  • 500+ projects delivered
  • Exporting to 30+ countries

Our Mission

To engineer steel products and services that empower contractors, builders and industrial manufacturers to deliver safer, faster and more efficient projects — without compromise.

Our Vision

To be the global benchmark for industrial steel manufacturing — a name synonymous with engineering rigor, uncompromising quality and the courage to deliver on tough deadlines.
