About Maalmo Steels

Engineered for Industry. Built to Perform.

Maalmo Steels is a vertically integrated industrial manufacturer delivering scaffolding systems, precision-cut steel components and end-to-end engineering services to some of the most demanding sectors in the world.

With an in-house facility covering design, laser cutting, forming, welding, electroplating and finishing — we control every stage of quality, ensuring our products meet international standards for safety and durability.

  • Vertically integrated plant
  • EN, BS & ASTM compliant
