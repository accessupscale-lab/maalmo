Engineering Product Painting

Coatings That Look as Good as They Last.

Our industrial painting line provides spray and dip painting services tailored to engineering products, structural steel, machinery and OEM parts. Every coating is applied over properly prepared substrates for maximum adhesion and durability.

From single-color batches to multi-coat protective systems, we manage colour matching, film thickness control and curing — all in a controlled, dust-managed environment.

  • Spray & dip processes
  • DFT-controlled application
  • Custom colour matching
  • Industrial-grade durability

Methods

Two Painting Capabilities

Spray Painting

Conventional and airless spray painting in controlled booths — ideal for finished surfaces requiring uniform film thickness and aesthetic finish.

  • Primer + topcoat systems
  • Custom RAL colour matching
  • Suitable for finished components
  • Indoor & outdoor grade

Dip Painting

Full-immersion dip painting for high throughput, complete coverage of complex shapes and economical batch processing of similar parts.

  • Full immersion coverage
  • Suitable for fasteners & brackets
  • High batch throughput
  • Cost-efficient on volume
