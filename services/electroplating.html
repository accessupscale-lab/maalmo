Electroplating / Galvanizing

Corrosion Protection That Outlasts the Conditions.

Our electroplating and galvanizing facility provides industrial-grade surface treatments engineered for steel components that face harsh weather, chemical exposure or marine environments.

From precision zinc plating of small fasteners to hot-dip galvanizing of structural components, we deliver consistent thickness, predictable adhesion and long-term performance.

  • Uniform coating thickness
  • RoHS-compliant chemistry
  • High-throughput line
  • Salt-spray tested
