CNC / Fiber Laser

Precision Cutting for Modern Fabrication.

Our optic fiber laser machines deliver clean, burr-free cuts across a wide range of stainless and mild steel thicknesses. Designed for high throughput and repeatable accuracy, our CNC laser capability powers everything from small batch prototypes to large production runs.

We combine high-speed fiber laser technology with experienced operators, CAD/CAM programming and a tight quality feedback loop — so every part ships cut-to-spec, first time.

  • ±0.1 mm tolerances
  • Up to 20 mm steel
  • CAD/CAM programming
  • Fast turnaround
