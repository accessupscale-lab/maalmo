Surface Preparation

The Foundation of Every Lasting Finish.

The longevity of any coating depends on what's beneath it. Our sand and shot blasting services strip rust, mill scale and contaminants — and create the precise surface profile your paint, galvanizing or specialty coating needs to truly bond.

Operating from an industrial blasting facility designed for safety and high throughput, we serve fabricators, OEMs, contractors and shipyards needing predictable, certifiable surface preparation.

  • SA 2.5 / SA 3 finishes
  • Controlled surface profile
  • Large parts handled
  • Ready for coating

Capabilities

Two Methods, One Goal

Sand Blasting

Abrasive blasting using fine media for thorough cleaning of contaminants, paint, rust and millscale. Ideal where surface aesthetics and tighter profiles matter.

  • SA 2.5 / SA 3 cleanliness
  • Aggregate & garnet media
  • Suitable for thin sections

Shot Blasting

Steel-shot blasting that delivers high-speed surface impact for heavy steel structures, large fabrications and machinery components.

  • Steel shot & grit media
  • High productivity
  • Heavy structural sections
