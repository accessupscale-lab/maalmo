Engineering

Bespoke Manufacturing. Engineered at Scale.

Our engineering division partners with OEMs, contractors and project developers to deliver custom steel components and complete Pre-Engineered Building (PEB) systems — backed by in-house design, certified fabrication and rigorous quality control.

Whether you need a single complex part or a turnkey PEB structure, we bring the engineering depth, production capacity and finishing capability to turn your drawings into shipped product.

  • In-house design team
  • Fabrication to EN/BS/ASTM
  • Load-tested components
  • End-to-end project delivery

Capabilities

Two Engineering Verticals

Specialized divisions built around the needs of modern industry.

Bespoke Custom Manufacturing

Engineered-to-order steel components for specialized industrial, infrastructure and OEM applications. We take your drawings — or co-develop them with you — and deliver finished parts ready to install.

  • Heavy structural fabrication
  • Weldments & subassemblies
  • Precision machined parts
  • Prototype to production runs

Pre-Engineered Building (PEB) 360 Solutions

Complete PEB packages — design, supply, finish and support. From warehouses and industrial sheds to processing plants, our PEB 360 offering covers the full lifecycle of your building.

  • Structural design & analysis
  • Primary & secondary framing
  • Roofing & cladding systems
  • Erection support & supervision
