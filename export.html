Export Process

From Enquiry to Delivery

A transparent, milestone-driven process that keeps your project on schedule.

01

Enquiry & Quote

Send us your BOQ, drawings or product list. We revert within 24 hours with a commercial quote, lead time and Incoterm options.

02

Order Confirmation

Purchase order and advance received. Production slot locked. Pre-production samples shared if required.

03

Production & QC

Manufacture to specification. Mill test reports, dimensional checks and galvanization inspection before dispatch.

04

Containerization & Shipping

Stuffed at our yard or CFS. B/L issued. Final documents couriered. Shipment tracked until destination.
